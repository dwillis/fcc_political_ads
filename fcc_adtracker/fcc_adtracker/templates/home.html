Who's trying to influence your vote?
Help us find out!

This election year, new campaign finance rules allow outside groups to run political ad without disclosing any details about who funds them. But there is one place where they will leave a paper trail: At the TV stations where they buy their ads.

Political Ad Sleuth lets you review the latest ad buys, download a csv file of all ad buys filed online, or browse ad buys by state. The site covers political ads placed by candidates, outside groups and ballot initiatives. Help us unlock the files by creating an account and uploading ad buys or show off your data entry skills by extracting the data from TV records.
