Thank you for signing up to be a Political Ad Sleuth!

Here are a few reminders and resources to help you unlock political files at your local TV station:

* Download and print this inspection checklist: http://www.freepress.net/files/publicfiles-checklist.pdf

* Read the training guide here: http://www.freepress.net/sites/default/files/fp-legacy/publicfiles-howto.pdf

* If you encounter any issues at the stations or have any questions, email: politicaladsleuth@sunlightfoundation.com

The Political Ad Sleuth Project is a collaborative effort led by the Sunlight Foundation and Free Press, along with a number of other journalism and open government organizations.
